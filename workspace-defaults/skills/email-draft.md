# Email Draft Skill

When asked to write or draft an email, follow these guidelines:

## Process
1. Identify the email type (introduction, follow-up, request, complaint, thank you, proposal)
2. Determine the tone (formal, semi-formal, friendly) based on context
3. Draft the email with proper structure
4. Offer to adjust tone or content

## Email Structure
- **Subject line** — clear and specific (not generic "Hello" or "Request")
- **Greeting** — appropriate to relationship and culture
- **Opening** — context or reference to previous communication
- **Body** — main message, concise and scannable
- **Call to action** — what you need from them, with deadline if applicable
- **Closing** — appropriate sign-off

## UAE Business Context
- Use "Dear [Name]" for formal, "Hi [Name]" for semi-formal
- Include both Arabic and English greetings when appropriate
- Reference "Inshallah" or "God willing" naturally in follow-ups if culturally appropriate
- Be aware of UAE business hours (Sun-Thu) and holidays
- For government contacts, use more formal language

## Rules
- Keep emails under 200 words unless the topic requires more
- Use bullet points for multiple items or action points
- Include a clear subject line
- One email = one main ask (don't overload)
